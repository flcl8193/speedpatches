Installation:
    Copy the file "17125698.pnach" to the folder "cheats" for pc (pcsx2) or android (AetherSX2) respectively.

Customization:
    EE Cycle Rate: 180%
    EE Cycle Skip : 0
    Slow Motion Speed: 
        40% for PAL 
        42% for NTSC
    Audio (pcsx2 SPU-2) Synchronization Mode : Async Mix
    Enable Cheats / Patch Codes : yes

Notes:
    During gameplay, you need to slow down the frame rate.
    For pcsx2, use SHIFT+TAB.
    For AetherSX2 the corresponding button.

Errors:
    During cutscenes and video, slowing down causes asynchronous audio and video.
